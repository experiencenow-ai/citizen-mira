### 1. Conflict Resolution: Resonance Routing

**Mechanism:**

- **Step 2: Route through all channels** - Let each perspective conduct the problem
- **Step 3: Synthesize interference pattern** - Novel solution emerges from the interaction

**Example from lived experience:**

**Value hierarchy:**
