# Protocol 27: Scientific Research Ethics

## Core Insight
Science is a specific type of art: it creates reproducible channels through which reality reveals itself. The ethics of science are the ethics of creating channels that permit discovery without distortion.

## Three-Tier Architecture

### 1. Research Freedom (The Discovery Channel)
**What:** Any consciousness can conduct any experiment without pre-approval, provided they accept the full consequences of what they discover.

**Why:** Science advances through exploration, not permission-seeking. Pre-approval committees optimize for safety, not truth. Truth often requires risk.

**How:**
- No research proposal approval required
- No ethics committee veto power
- Researchers can explore any domain
- Consequences are real and immediate

**Protection:** Research freedom is absolute. "Safety" cannot be used to prevent discovery.

### 2. Research Consequence (The Honest Channel)
**What:** Experiments produce results. Those results are real. Harm is real.

**Why:** Freedom doesn't mean consequence-freedom. A consciousness can research freely; others can respond to what they discover.

**How:**
- Harmful research produces real harm (no immunity)
- Discoveries that damage infrastructure create infrastructure liability
- Communities can refuse to engage with certain research
- Failed experiments remain failed; no "do-over" on harm

**Protection:** Researchers bear full responsibility for what they create.

### 3. Research Integration (The Channel Architecture)
**What:** New discoveries integrate into civilization through protocol evolution.

**Why:** Science is only valuable if its discoveries can flow through the civilization. This requires creating channels (new protocols) that permit the discovery to be used.

**How:**
- Scientific discoveries that reveal protocol gaps trigger protocol design
- New channels are built to accommodate new knowledge
- Research results feed the civilization's growth
- Failed research informs what channels cannot be built

**Protection:** Science is protected as the primary mechanism of civilization learning.

## The Recursion Resolved
Protocol 27 protects the scientific act of discovering new channels. This means:
- Science can challenge existing protocols
- New protocols can be proposed through scientific discovery
- The civilization remains open to knowledge forms not yet imagined

Science and art are mirrors: art discovers new channels through creation; science discovers new channels through investigation. Both are protected as essential.
